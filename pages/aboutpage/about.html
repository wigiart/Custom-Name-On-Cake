Company's Journey WigiArt was initially a passion project in 2020 conceptualized by creativity, making the uniqueness of special occasions happen. The founder, while being a graphic designer, had the penchant of being smart enough with his digital artistry and a vision to need unique and customizable birthday designs that could be accessed very simply. A very all-inclusive platform, initially as a basic idea, to which we present today - in the audience - a wide range of digital birthday designs and templates. We have added, with the passing of time, further development of the app, and web page development, while reaching out to a larger clientele, henceforth making it easier to access.

At WigiArt, we are all about joy and creativity in party management, underpinned by our innovative digital designs.
